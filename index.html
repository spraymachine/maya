About Me

Dedicated to excellence in mathematics, statistics, and research

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at GITAM School of Science, GITAM (Deemed to be) University, Visakhapatnam. With over 24 years of teaching experience and a strong research background, I specialize in Operations Research, Statistics, and Quality Control.

My research focuses on optimization of reliability systems, with particular expertise in k-out-of-n integrated redundant reliability systems. I have published 26 research papers, with 21 indexed publications in reputable journals.
